Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about Skill Swap

How do I create a skill listing?
To create a skill listing, go to your dashboard and click "Add New Skill". Fill in the details about what you can teach, your experience level, availability, and preferred teaching mode. Once submitted, your skill will be visible to other users.
How does the payment system work?
Skill Swap uses a secure payment system where learners pay for sessions upfront. Funds are held securely and released to teachers after completed sessions. We support major credit cards, PayPal, and bank transfers.
What if I need to cancel a session?
You can cancel sessions up to 24 hours in advance without penalty. For cancellations within 24 hours, please contact the other user directly to reschedule. Repeated last-minute cancellations may affect your account standing.
How do I verify my teaching credentials?
You can verify your credentials by uploading certificates, diplomas, or other proof of expertise in your profile settings. Our team reviews submissions within 2 business days. Verified teachers receive a badge on their profile.
Is my personal information secure?
Yes, we take data security seriously. All personal information is encrypted and stored securely. We never share your data with third parties without your consent. You can read more in our Privacy Policy.
What happens if I have a dispute with another user?
If you have a dispute, please contact our support team immediately. We'll mediate the situation and work towards a fair resolution. For payment disputes, we can temporarily hold funds while investigating the issue.
